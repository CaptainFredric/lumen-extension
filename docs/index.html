Workflow

From page to artifact to action.

Capture

Clean the page before it is stitched.

Lumen removes floating interference, wakes deferred content, and captures the page in slices that hold up on retina displays.

Inspect

Extract the page system, not just the bitmap.

Palette, typography, layout density, headline shape, navigation labels, and CTA language are promoted into a reusable blueprint.

Studio

Turn evidence into a presentable asset.

The next layer packages the capture into browser frames, mobile mockups, annotated explainers, and redacted client-safe exports.

Archive

Keep a timeline, not a folder of dead PNGs.

Sync, scheduled watchlists, and visual diffing move Lumen toward the real SaaS product: a system of monitored web surfaces.

Who pays

The value is strongest where capture is tied to a downstream decision.

Design teams and agencies

They need clean references, mood boards, and structured brand cues. Blueprint extraction and studio exports make the screenshot usable immediately.

Marketers and copywriters

They build swipe files and campaign archives. Clean capture plus sync and watch automation becomes a repeatable research system.

Founders and product operators

They care about changes in competitor positioning and pricing. The monitor product is the premium tier, not the screenshot itself.

QA and developers

They need fast evidence, redaction, and issue-ready exports. Lumen is strongest when it shortens the path from bug report to action.
